Our Vision

Here at Pehchan-Your Unique Identity, we know that sometimes all it takes to change the world is a little support. Since our start in 2020, we have been determined to make an impact. The core of our efforts is to bring our team's fresh ideas and passion to the range of activities we're involved in. PEHCHAN is working for social welfare as a team, which was formed by Aniksha Upadhyay & Team in April 15,2020 It works on several causes Awareness, Children Education, Environment, Animals, helpless people and Community Welfare.

Our team is dedicated to service for poor public welfare and animal welfare.
